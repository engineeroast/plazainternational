ABOUT THE HOTEL

Our long-term vision is to be the hotel of choice for guests.

  • We deliver memorable moments every day, every time.
  • We enjoy serving with our “Yes I Can!” spirit.

Hotel Plaza International is one of the leading hotels of the city that works as a true host and best partner to guests.
